## VIDEO DISTRIBUTION GUIDE

### **Where to Post**
- YouTube (main channel)
- Website homepage
- Email campaigns
- Social media (native + links)
- Help center/FAQ section
- Product onboarding

### **Optimization Tips**
- Add captions (90% watch without sound)
- Include timestamps in description
- Add call-to-action at end
- Create thumbnail with faces/emotions
- Use compelling title ("Revealed:" "Finally:" "Exactly:")

### **Expected Engagement**
- Subscribe rate: 5-10% of viewers
- View duration: 70%+ of total length
- Click-through rate: 15-25% on CTAs
