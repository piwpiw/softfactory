## Executive Overview

SoftFactory launches with a **4-phase go-to-market strategy** designed to:
1. **Build momentum** (early adopters, brand awareness)
2. **Scale efficiently** (organic growth, partnerships, paid)
3. **Establish category** (thought leadership, integrations)
4. **Drive enterprise** (sales team, strategic accounts)

**Target outcomes:**
- Month 1: 500 signups, 100 paying customers
- Month 3: 2,000 signups, 500 paying customers
- Month 6: 8,000 signups, 3,000 paying customers
- Month 12: 20,000 signups, 10,000 paying customers
