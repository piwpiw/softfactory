## COOCOOK SERVICE

### Booking & Reservations

**Q: What time can I book a chef?**
**A:** Flexible! We have chefs available for breakfasts (6-9am), lunches (11am-2pm), dinners (5-9pm), and all-day services. Book 24+ hours in advance for guaranteed availability.

**Q: How much does it cost?**
**A:** Subscription is $29/month. Per-chef rates vary ($50-$500/hour). See chef profile for pricing.

**Q: Can I book the same chef again?**
**A:** Yes! After first booking, save them as favorite. They're prioritized for your future bookings. Many offer loyalty discounts after 3+ bookings.

**Q: Can I cancel a booking?**
**A:**
- 48+ hours before: Full refund
- 24-48 hours: 50% refund
- <24 hours: No refund (chef might reject or you forfeit)

**Q: What if my chosen chef cancels?**
**A:** Rare, but it happens. You get:
1. Full refund immediately
2. Offer to book alternative chef (similar rating)
3. $50 credit for inconvenience

**Q: Can I request a specific chef?**
**A:** Yes! Click chef profile â†’ "Favorite" â†’ "Request for date/time". They'll respond within 12 hours.

**Q: Do I need to provide ingredients?**
**A:** No. Most chefs bring ingredients or you can request they buy them (cost added to bill). Discuss in chef's chat before booking.

**Q: Can I request a custom menu?**
**A:** Absolutely! In the "Special Requests" field, describe your ideal menu. Chef will confirm before service.

### Chef Quality & Safety

**Q: Are chefs vetted?**
**A:** Yes! Every chef goes through background check, credential verification, and insurance check. Only 15% of applicants are approved.

**Q: Do chefs have insurance?**
**A:** Yes. Every chef carries liability insurance. If anything breaks, insurance covers it.

**Q: What if I'm allergic to something?**
**A:** Tell us in the "Dietary Requirements" field. Also tell the chef directly in chat. Chefs take this seriously.

**Q: What's the chef rating system?**
**A:** 5-star system. Based on customer reviews. Look for:
- 4.5+ = Excellent choice
- 4.0-4.5 = Very good
- 3.5-4.0 = Good
- <3.5 = Proceed carefully

**Q: Can I see chef reviews before booking?**
**A:** Yes! Each chef's profile shows 5-10 most recent reviews. Click "View All Reviews" for complete list.

### During Service

**Q: What should I have ready before the chef arrives?**
**A:**
- Clean kitchen
- Gas/electric stove working
- Refrigerator space for ingredients
- All dishes/utensils accessible
- Trash & compost ready

**Q: Can I cook alongside the chef?**
**A:** Yes! Many customers do. Some prefer to let chef do it solo. Discuss with chef before.

**Q: What if something goes wrong during service?**
**A:** Message chef immediately. For emergencies, call our support team 24/7.

**Q: How long does cleanup take?**
**A:** Usually included in service time. Chef cleans kitchen after cooking. Ask about cleanup specifics before booking.

### Payment

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
**A:** Credit card (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, bank transfer.

**Q: When am I charged?**
**A:** When you confirm booking (not when service happens). Chef confirms 24 hours before.

**Q: Can I tip the chef?**
**A:** Yes! You can add tip (15-20% recommended) after service through the platform. Direct cash also welcome.

## REVIEW SERVICE

### Creating Campaigns

**Q: How do I start a campaign?**
**A:** Click "Create Campaign" â†’ Fill details (name, description, product category, reviewer count, budget, timeline) â†’ Approve reviewers â†’ Done.

**Q: How long should my campaign run?**
**A:** 4-6 weeks minimum. Product testing (2-4 weeks) + review submission (1-2 weeks).

**Q: How many reviewers should I get?**
**A:** 10-20 for good diversity. More = better coverage, but cost increases. 15 is the sweet spot.

**Q: What's fair compensation for reviewers?**
**A:**
- Free product only: Best for influencers
- $50-$100: Micro-influencers (5K-50K followers)
- $200+: Major influencers (100K+ followers)

**Q: Should I pay reviewers?**
**A:** Definitely! It shows you value their time. Unpaid campaigns = lower-quality reviews + fewer applicants.

**Q: What should I request in reviews?**
**A:** Be specific! Don't say "tell us what you think". Say "please comment on taste, texture, packaging, and whether you'd recommend".

### Managing Applications

**Q: How do I choose reviewers?**
**A:** Look at:
- Follower count (bigger = more reach)
- Quality rating (4.5+ = good)
- Niche relevance (food bloggers for food products)
- Past reviews (detailed = better)

**Q: Can I reject applicants?**
**A:** Yes! No reason required. You can message them explaining why (optional, good etiquette).

**Q: How do rejected reviewers react?**
**A:** Most understand. Some ask why. Be kind - they might apply for future campaigns.

**Q: Can I reach out to reviewers without campaigns?**
**A:** Not directly. But you can approve them, message via platform, and build relationships.

### Products & Shipping

**Q: Do I ship products to reviewers?**
**A:** Yes, you do. Platform handles payment, you arrange logistics.

**Q: What if a reviewer's address is wrong?**
**A:** Reach out through platform message. Correct address. Reship if needed.

**Q: What if product doesn't arrive?**
**A:** Message reviewer. You may need to reship. Track shipment on your end.

### Reviews & Results

**Q: What if I get bad reviews?**
**A:** That's real feedback! Don't ask reviewers to remove or revise unless factually wrong. Use feedback to improve.

**Q: Can reviewers edit reviews?**
**A:** Yes, before campaign end date. After that, reviews are final.

**Q: How do I use reviews in marketing?**
**A:** Feature on website, share on social media, include in ads (get permission), add to email campaigns, use in pitch decks.

**Q: Can I request more details from a reviewer?**
**A:** Yes! Message them asking for clarification. Most will oblige.

**Q: How many reviews typically come back?**
**A:** 70-90%. Some reviewers ghost. Approve backup reviewers or add more if needed.

## AI AUTOMATION SERVICE

### Building Workflows

**Q: Do I need to code to build automations?**
**A:** No! Fully visual, drag-and-drop builder. No code skills required.

**Q: What's the hardest workflow to build?**
**A:** Complex multi-condition workflows. Start simple. Build up complexity over time.

**Q: How many workflows can I create?**
**A:** Unlimited! No limit on automations.

**Q: What's a "trigger"?**
**A:** The event that starts a workflow. Email received, form submitted, time reached, webhook received, etc.

**Q: What's an "action"?**
**A:** What happens after trigger. Send message, create record, update database, make API call, etc.

**Q: Can a workflow trigger another workflow?**
**A:** Yes! Use webhooks. Workflow A completion triggers Workflow B. Create chains of automations.

### Execution & Reliability

**Q: How reliable are automations?**
**A:** 99.9% uptime. Millions of executions daily. Enterprise-grade infrastructure.

**Q: What if an automation fails?**
**A:** You get notification. Check execution logs to see error. Fix and re-run manually if needed.

**Q: Can automations retry?**
**A:** Yes! Configure retry logic (3 retries, with delays). For critical workflows, always set retries.

**Q: How fast do automations execute?**
**A:** Usually <5 seconds. Instant in most cases. Webhooks are real-time.

**Q: How many automations can run simultaneously?**
**A:** Unlimited! Runs in parallel, not sequentially.

### Variables & Data

**Q: What are "variables"?**
**A:** Placeholders that pull real data into your automation. `{{ email.from }}` pulls sender's email address.

**Q: How do I use variables?**
**A:** Type `{{ variable_name }}`. System suggests options. Data auto-fills when automation runs.

**Q: Can I format/transform data?**
**A:** Yes! Use filters: `{{ email.body | first_100_chars }}` gets first 100 characters.

**Q: What if data is empty?**
**A:** Set fallback/default value. Example: `{{ customer_name | default: "Valued Customer" }}`

### Templates

**Q: Are there pre-built templates?**
**A:** Yes! 50+ templates for common workflows (email forwarding, form to CRM, scheduled reports, etc.).

**Q: Can I modify templates?**
**A:** Yes! Start with template â†’ Customize â†’ Save as your own.

**Q: Can I share templates with team?**
**A:** Yes, within your workspace. Team members can duplicate and customize.
