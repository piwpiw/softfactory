## Messaging Do's and Don'ts

### DO:
- Lead with customer outcome (time saved, cost cut)
- Use specific numbers ($29/mo, 6 hours, 70% automated)
- Mention the integrated benefit (one platform advantage)
- Include proof (case studies, data, testimonials)
- Speak to service entrepreneur pain points
- Use conversational, relatable language

### DON'T:
- Use generic "automation platform" framing
- Compare feature-by-feature with Zapier (you lose on scale)
- Say "startup" (target service business, not tech startup)
- Mention technical details (API, webhook, etc.) in consumer copy
- Overstate AI capabilities (be specific: customer service, content, lead qualification)
- Use jargon (no "workflow," "automation," "integration" for general audience)
