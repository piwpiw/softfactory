## What to Do If You're Not Ready

### If product isn't ready:
- **DON'T LAUNCH YET.** Slipping a week is better than launching broken.
- Fix the issues, then launch when ready.
- You only get one launch day.

### If team isn't aligned:
- **STOP.** Do alignment sprint before launch.
- Everyone must understand positioning, messaging, pricing.
- Misalignment will be visible to customers.

### If you don't have marketing assets:
- **Simplify.** You need: landing page, one-pager, pitch deck.
- Video testimonials can come later.
- Get these three things perfect first.

### If CAC budget is constrained:
- **FOCUS on organic.** Shift from paid to content + partnerships.
- Scrappy growth beats no growth.
- Paid comes later when you have proof.
