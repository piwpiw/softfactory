  - **Product Hunt & Communities:** 15% of customers, $5 CAC (launch + communities)
  - **Paid Advertising:** 27% of customers, $35 CAC (Google, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube)

## Risk Assessment

### Risk 1: Slower Adoption Than Projected
**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** High
**Mitigation:** Increase paid advertising budget by 50%, double influencer spending, survey customers for messaging issues

### Risk 2: Stronger Competitive Response
**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** Medium
**Mitigation:** Differentiate on service-specific features, expand to adjacent verticals (fitness, beauty, photography), build switching costs

### Risk 3: Product Defects
**Probability:** Low | **Impact:** High
**Mitigation:** Extensive QA testing (80%+ test coverage), 24/7 monitoring, rapid incident response

### Risk 4: Fundraising Challenges
**Probability:** Low (Unit econ strong) | **Impact:** High
**Mitigation:** Bootstrap contingency plan if needed, focus on profitability earlier

### Risk 5: Key Team Member Departure
**Probability:** Low | **Impact:** Medium
**Mitigation:** Cross-training, documentation, competitive compensation

### What to Do Immediately

1. **Validate product readiness** (1 week)
   - Run final load test
   - QA all 5 services end-to-end
   - Verify payment processing

2. **Finalize marketing materials** (1 week)
   - Website review + final polish
   - Product Hunt listing finalized
   - Email sequences ready to send

3. **Brief the team** (2 days)
   - Clear roles for launch week
   - On-call coverage 24/7
   - Escalation process

4. **Reach out to influencers** (3 days)
   - 50 nano-influencers (early access preview)
   - 10 macro-influencers (sample accounts)
   - Ask for Product Hunt support on day 1

5. **Go, go, go!** (Feb 25 at 10am PST)
   - Submit to Product Hunt
   - Email blast to warm list
   - Twitter storm
   - Founder answering questions all day

### What NOT to Do

- ❌ Launch before Feb 25 (lose momentum, dilute messaging)
- ❌ Over-promise features we don't have
- ❌ Price lower than $29 (trains customers to expect cheap, hurts margins)
- ❌ Try to be everything for everyone (focus on service entrepreneurs first)
- ❌ Skimp on customer support (retention is critical early)
